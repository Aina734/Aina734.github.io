About Me

I love to examine plants and animals, I was able to learn a bit more deeply about plants family naming and decide to search about it's similarities, uses (including in tales and fantasy settings) I find having a hobby to be great at easing tension and quite informative

Drawing is really fun and it's a great observation exercise, trying to get an idea down in a file or paper it's rewarding, you kind of zoom out at times but leaving some creativity run wild while figuring out how to make out an effect like fog or flames, how does the things around the drawing are affected. I put more time into digital color application but I also enjoy the sketch, it's also great to compare how you progress with the practice you put into it

Game Dev is something I still cosidere a hobby, I haven't got enough practice to actually completely build something and put it out there, but is a hobby that I hope to turn into a goal, it get frustrating but being able to accomplish little things it's truly rewarding. Music composition it's also nice (I know why music composition so center into boring music theory) well you will be surprise, music theory might no be a easy, but it has so much potential so instead of learning (torturing someone) violin I find it more useful to learn the building blocks and apply them with a DAWs (Digital Audio Workstation). It feel more or less like drawing, like trying to explain a something (experience-situation-emotion and so on) with sounds and rhythms

I support the though of integral individuals who can develop beyond what is expected in our common enviroment (work-social-cricle I think you get it already) so Hurray for everyone who decide to embark in this wonderful life-long tarvel of knowledge adquisition with all it's hurdles and challenges!!
